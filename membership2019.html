Membership Drive 2019

A membership drive was organized by The Computer Society of India, SFIT â€“ Student Chapter during the last week of July and the first week of August for SE, TE and FE students respectively. The core objective of the drive was to encourage students to reap the benefits of CSI membership which include access to CSI journals and magazines, privilege to publish articles in the CSI journal and attend free seminars and workshops organized by the chapter.
The committee members of CSI told the students which workshops they can expect from CSI in the academic year 2019-2020. Demonstration of Website development, Video Editing and Ethical Hacking were shown to the students so that they can have a clear idea about the upcoming workshops.
An overwhelming response was received from SE and FE students. 236 (total number of students who enrolled) students of which 147 (no of enrolled Fe students) belong to the FE department and 89 (no of enrolled SE and TE students) to the SE and TE department opted for CSI membership. The drive was a successful event that has enabled CSI to reach out to a wider audience.
