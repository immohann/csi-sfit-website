Seminar 2019

The Computer Society of India (CSI), SFIT student chapter organized a seminar on Machine Learning , Python , AWS Certification Course and Internship on 28th September, 2019 . The seminar was conducted by Mr. Junaid Khateeb and Mr. Rohit Sharma. It was free of cost and was attended by CSI members. Ms Amrita Mathur, CSI- SFIT faculty coordinator supervised the seminar. The seminar started at 11:00 pm in the TPO office. It focused on the following points: A small introduction on python and the importance of python for data science ,a small introduction on Machine learning Importance of certification courses, Internship opportunities for both python and Machine learning and Guidance about Amazon web services certification courses. At the end of the session, Ms Amrita Mathur thanked Mr. Junaid Khateeb and Mr. Rohit Sharma on behalf of the CSI- SFIT team. The seminar was appreciated by all the attendees. It was concluded at 1:00pm. ROHIT SHARMA:Microsoft & Amazon web services employee
