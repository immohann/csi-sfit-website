On 3rd March 2017, CSI organized an informative seminar on ‘Technical Paper Writing and Presentation.' The one hour seminar was conducted by Dr. Sincy George (Principal, SFIT).Topics such as the prerequisites for writing a technical paper, the dos and don’ts while drafting and how the content should be remodeled and presented as per different target audience were explained. Questions and doubts of students regarding this topic were skillfully resolved by Dr. Sincy George. Amrita Mathur (Assistant Professor-SFIT) gave vote of thanks and concluded the workshop.
