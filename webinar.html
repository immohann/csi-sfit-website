CSI Webinar 2019

The seminar was taken by Master Harsh Jalan who was the ex-president of CSI. He has taught ML/AI to over 1500 students. His team won at the SIH 2019 at SFIT where they built a multilingual conversational chatbot. He recently presented a paper titled Suspect Face Generation at CSCITA 2020 by IEEE where it won the Best Paper award. He is currently in the process of writing a book chapter titled Understanding and Building Generative Adversarial Networks for a book titled Handbook of Research in Machine Learning The seminar started by explaining what Machine Learning exactly is and what we do in it with the help of some practical examples. He then moved on to the differences and similarities about Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. He also talked about Deep Learning which is a subfield of Machine Learning and Neuro Symbolic AI which is a new and emerging field with a lot of potential in the future. He then explained about the different applications of AI and ML and also their career paths. There was a discussion about the work flow of ML and how to go about starting ML. As ML and AI as they require a high computing power with GPUs and CPUs we were informed about the different platforms which are available for it like Google Colab, Kaggle, AWS, Google Cloud, etc. There was then a doubt solving session after which the seminar was concluded.
