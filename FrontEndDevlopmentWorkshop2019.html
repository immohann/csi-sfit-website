Front End Devlopment Workshop was organised by CSI on the 12th and 13th of October. It was conducted by CSI-Chairperson Jayashree Domala along with CSI-Joint Tech Head Manmohan Dogra . The first session covered topics ranging from Introduction to Front End Devlopment starting with html upto Css. On the second day,Topics covered were bootsrap and projects on Front End Devlopment. Students were given certificates .
